How to buy

Download & setup MetaMask or TrustWallet

Download MetaMask (a crypto wallet in form of a browser extension) or TrustWallet (an app for your phone). After that you will have to add the Binance Smart Chain to your network-list. (Click here for a step-by-step tutorial)

Buy and send BNB to MetaMask

Buy BNB on an exchange (i.e. Binance, Kraken, Coinbase etc.). Transfer the tokens to your MetaMask wallet address. BEP-20 addresses start with a "0x"

Head on to PancakeSwap and swap for $PITBULLRUN

Click here to head on over to PancakeSwap or use this address 0x1889f1ae46c2da9e8f3532a21cc4719e2db35f7b to select PITBULLRUN. Set the slippage tolerance to 10% (sometimes it may be a bit more, depending on how much demand there is)

View $PITBULLRUN and HODL

Swap BNB for PITBULLRUN. Now you need to add PITBULLRUN to your MetaMask (Tutorial here) or Trust Wallet to view your $PITBULLRUN. Lastly HODL!
